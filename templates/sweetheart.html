House Sweet Heart

Every year, the Sigma Phi Delta brothers cast their vote for a new house sweet heart. A house sweet heart is the female face of the fraternity. She holds important roles in representing the fraternity and helping advance the object of the Sigma Phi Delta fraternity.

Kristina Lynch

A second year Chemistry major, Kristina Lynch, also a member of Delta Zeta sorority, was voted by the brothers as the house sweet heart
Miss. Lynch has shown tremendous commitment to the Beta Lambda chapter. From supporting in events both socially and philanthropically, to surprising the brothers with home baked cookies during finals week, Kristina is always willing to help however she can.
Kristina lives in the Delta Zeta house and serves on the Executive board as the Vice President of Membership Education, this on top of her full schedule of rigorous Chemistry coursework.
Its wonderful to see her impressive commitment to the well being of our brothers and brotherhood. We are extremely thankful for her continued support and wish to honor her with the title of Sigma Phi Delta House Sweet Heart.
