about us

On April 20, 2013, the Sigma Phi Delta - Beta Lambda (SDSU) chapter was founded. Ever since, the engineering brotherhood has grown in terms of philanthropy and professsional achievement. Adjoined by other sororities, the Sigma Phi Delta brothers hold various philantrhopy events across the community, such as beach clean ups, blood drives and numerious fundraising events. Professionally, the SDSU chapter has toured many local engineering companies across San Diego and owns a Robo^TM 3D printer to help the brothers envision their 3D designs.
